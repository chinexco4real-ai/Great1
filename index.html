Dual login auto-switch
Alternates between two tokens. Login 1 runs until it hits its loss limit, then the EA resets its session and switches to Login 2. Login 2 runs until it hits its profit target, then switches back to Login 1 — back and forth, non-stop.

Login 2 timer close
Starts a countdown the moment Login 2 becomes active. Login 2 switches back to Login 1 on whichever happens first: the profit target above, or this countdown running out.
If the profit target hasn't been reached when this runs out, the EA attempts to close any open trade and switches back to Login 1.
